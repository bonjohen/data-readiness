Training and inference data governance addresses the unique risks that come with managing datasets used to train, fine-tune, or provide context to AI models. Without clear lineage and approval processes, organizations risk using unauthorized data sources, retaining sensitive data beyond policy limits, or losing track of what data influenced model behavior.
