Audit Support provides hands-on assistance during your PCI DSS assessment, whether you are completing a SAQ with an Independent Security Assessor or undergoing a full ROC with a QSA. We assemble your evidence package — organizing artifacts by requirement number so the assessor can efficiently validate each control — and coordinate the logistics of penetration testing, ASV scans, and on-site interviews.

When gaps are discovered during the assessment, we help document compensating controls or exceptions with the rigor QSAs expect: a description of the constraint preventing compliance with the stated requirement, the objective of the original requirement, the compensating control that meets that objective, and the risk analysis demonstrating that the compensating control provides equivalent protection. This documentation is often the difference between a finding and a clean report.
