The Remediation Program takes the gaps identified in the Readiness Sprint and closes them systematically. We work alongside your IT and security teams to implement the technical and procedural controls required for PCI DSS compliance, prioritizing the highest-risk gaps first. Network segmentation reduces your CDE scope and assessment complexity, encryption protects data at rest and in transit, and access controls ensure only authorized personnel can reach cardholder data.

The program includes creation of all required policies, procedures, and operational documentation — from the overarching information security policy (Req 12) through specific technical procedures for patch management (Req 6), log review (Req 10), and incident response. Every deliverable is designed to serve double duty: it closes the compliance gap and becomes reusable evidence for your QSA assessment.
