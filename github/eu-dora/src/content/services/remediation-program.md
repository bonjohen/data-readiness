The Remediation Program takes the findings from a readiness assessment and converts them into operational controls, policies, and procedures that satisfy DORA's requirements. We work alongside your ICT risk, security, and compliance teams to build or enhance the specific artifacts that competent authorities expect to see — from the ICT risk management framework document through the third-party ICT register to tested BCP/DR plans.

The programme is modular: organizations can engage on specific domains (e.g., third-party ICT risk only, or incident reporting readiness only) or run a comprehensive remediation covering all nine control areas. Each deliverable is designed to be operationally sustainable, not just audit-ready — the procedures and registers we build are meant to be maintained by your teams after the engagement concludes.
