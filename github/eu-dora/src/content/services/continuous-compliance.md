DORA compliance is not a one-time achievement — the regulation requires ongoing maintenance of frameworks, registers, testing programmes, and governance practices. Our continuous compliance service provides a dedicated resource to keep your DORA posture current through quarterly reviews, annual testing coordination, and real-time tracking of regulatory developments including new RTS/ITS delegated acts and supervisory expectations.

The service is designed for organizations that have completed initial remediation and need sustainable, cost-effective support to maintain compliance without building a full-time in-house DORA programme. We act as an extension of your compliance and ICT risk teams, handling the recurring administrative and analytical workload so your internal staff can focus on operational priorities.
