The Remediation Program takes the findings from a Readiness Sprint or equivalent assessment and systematically closes the identified gaps. Over four to twelve weeks — depending on the scope and complexity of your processing landscape — we implement the policies, processes, and technical controls needed to achieve and demonstrate GDPR compliance. Each deliverable is designed to be operationally sustainable, not a one-time compliance artifact that decays the moment the engagement ends.

The program covers the highest-impact compliance areas: transparency and privacy notices, data subject rights fulfillment, consent management, processor governance, impact assessments, and breach preparedness. We work alongside your legal, IT, and operations teams to embed these capabilities into existing workflows rather than creating parallel compliance processes. The result is a compliance infrastructure that your team can maintain, update, and audit without ongoing external dependency — though many organizations choose to follow the Remediation Program with our Continuous Compliance engagement for sustained support.
