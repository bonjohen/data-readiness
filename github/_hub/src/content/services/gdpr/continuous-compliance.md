GDPR compliance is not a project with an end date — it is an ongoing operational requirement that demands continuous attention to processing changes, regulatory developments, and organizational evolution. Our Continuous Compliance engagement provides a dedicated privacy operations team that monitors your compliance posture month over month, ensuring that the infrastructure built during readiness and remediation phases remains current and effective.

Each month, we track DSR response times against your SLA commitments, monitor regulatory developments from the EDPB and relevant member state authorities, and flag changes that require policy or process updates. Quarterly ROPA refreshes capture new processing activities introduced by product launches, vendor changes, or organizational restructuring. Annual cycles include DPIA reviews for ongoing high-risk processing, processor re-assessments to verify continued adequacy of technical and organizational measures, and a compliance posture report for senior management and board-level reporting.
