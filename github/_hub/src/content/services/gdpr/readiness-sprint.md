The GDPR Readiness Sprint provides a rapid, structured assessment of your organization's current compliance posture against the full scope of the General Data Protection Regulation. Over two to four weeks, our team maps your processing activities, documents lawful bases, traces data flows across systems and borders, and evaluates whether your organization requires a DPO. The sprint produces a clear-eyed gap analysis that tells you exactly where you stand — not where you hope you stand.

The output is a prioritized remediation roadmap that distinguishes between critical gaps (exposing you to enforcement risk), moderate gaps (requiring attention before your next processor audit or DPA inquiry), and low-priority improvements. This roadmap becomes the input for the Remediation Program engagement or for your internal team to execute independently. Organizations typically use the Readiness Sprint as the starting point for a GDPR compliance program or as a health check after significant organizational changes such as acquisitions, new product launches, or expansion into new EU member states.
