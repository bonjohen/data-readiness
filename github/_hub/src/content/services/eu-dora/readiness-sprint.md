The Readiness Sprint produces a clear picture of your organization's current position against DORA's requirements and a prioritized roadmap to close gaps before regulatory examination. In 2–4 weeks, we assess your ICT risk management framework, third-party ICT arrangements, incident classification and reporting readiness, resilience testing programme, and governance structures against the regulation's specific articles and the published RTS/ITS delegated acts.

The engagement concludes with a management body briefing that translates technical findings into governance-level decisions — where budget is needed, which gaps carry the most supervisory risk, and what timeline is realistic for achieving compliance. This briefing directly supports the Art. 5 requirement for management body oversight of ICT risk.
