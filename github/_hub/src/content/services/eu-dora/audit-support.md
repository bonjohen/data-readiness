When competent authorities examine your DORA compliance — or when your organization faces internal audit review of digital operational resilience — we provide hands-on support to assemble evidence, prepare stakeholders, and ensure that your compliance posture is presented accurately and completely. This includes organizing the ICT third-party register for regulatory submission, packaging resilience testing results, and preparing governance documentation.

We also coordinate with your teams to identify and address any last-mile gaps discovered during preparation, ensuring that examination findings reflect your actual control posture rather than documentation deficiencies. For financial entities subject to TLPT requirements, we support coordination with the competent authority and the testing teams throughout the exercise.
