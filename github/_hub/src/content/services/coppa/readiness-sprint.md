The Readiness Sprint delivers a comprehensive assessment of your organization's COPPA compliance posture in 2-4 weeks. We evaluate whether your sites and services qualify as "directed to children" or "mixed audience" under 16 CFR § 312.2, audit your privacy notices against the specific disclosure requirements of § 312.4, test your parental consent mechanisms for robustness and FTC acceptability, and review every third-party SDK and vendor relationship for operator liability exposure under § 312.8.

The engagement produces a risk-ranked gap analysis that prioritizes findings by enforcement likelihood and remediation effort, along with an executive readout that translates technical findings into business decisions. Organizations preparing for FTC inquiry, launching new child-directed features, or integrating COPPA requirements into existing privacy programs use the Readiness Sprint to establish a clear baseline and actionable remediation plan.
