Continuous Compliance keeps your multi-state privacy program current as the regulatory landscape evolves. New states are enacting comprehensive privacy laws at an accelerating pace, existing cure periods are sunsetting, and attorney general enforcement activity is increasing. This ongoing engagement provides monthly monitoring, quarterly reviews, and annual updates to ensure your compliance posture adapts to changes without requiring a new assessment cycle each time a state enacts or amends its privacy law.
