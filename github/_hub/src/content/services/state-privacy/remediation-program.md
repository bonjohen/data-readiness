The Remediation Program takes the findings from the Readiness Sprint and implements the changes needed to achieve multi-state privacy compliance. Over 4-12 weeks, we build the operational infrastructure — privacy notices, consumer rights workflows, universal opt-out mechanisms, processor agreements, and impact assessment processes — that enables your organization to meet its obligations under each applicable state law. The program is modular: if your organization already has strong capabilities in some areas, we focus effort on the gaps.
