Audit Support provides expert assistance when your organization faces an attorney general inquiry, civil investigative demand, or compliance examination related to state privacy law obligations. We coordinate the response effort, assemble evidence across multiple state requirements, manage cure period timelines where applicable, and ensure your documentation demonstrates compliance. This service is available on-demand during any regulatory interaction and scales based on the complexity and number of states involved.
