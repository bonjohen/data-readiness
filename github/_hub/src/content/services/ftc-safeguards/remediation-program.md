The Remediation Program closes the gaps identified in your readiness assessment by implementing the specific safeguards required by 16 CFR Part 314. We work alongside your team to build or strengthen each control area — from risk assessment methodology and access control architecture to encryption deployment, MFA rollout, and incident response planning — delivering working controls, not just documentation.

Each remediation workstream produces both the operational control and the evidence artifacts needed to demonstrate compliance. The program is structured so that the highest-risk gaps are addressed first, with progress tracked against the readiness assessment findings.
