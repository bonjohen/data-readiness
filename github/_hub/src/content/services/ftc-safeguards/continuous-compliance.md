FTC Safeguards Rule compliance is not a one-time achievement — the rule requires periodic risk assessment updates, annual board reporting, ongoing vendor oversight, and continuous monitoring of safeguards. Continuous Compliance keeps your compliance program running on schedule, your evidence current, and your institution prepared for examination at any time.

The monthly engagement includes tracking regulatory developments and FTC enforcement actions that may affect your compliance posture, ensuring your safeguards program evolves with both the regulatory landscape and your institution's operations.
