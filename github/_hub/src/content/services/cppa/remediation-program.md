The Remediation Program takes the findings from a readiness assessment and executes the fixes. Over four to twelve weeks depending on scope, we rewrite your privacy disclosures to meet statutory requirements, build or configure your consumer request intake and fulfillment workflows, amend vendor agreements, establish retention and disposal policies, and deploy the technical mechanisms (GPC recognition, opt-out links, SPI limitation controls) that the statute requires.

Each deliverable is built to be operationally sustainable, not just legally compliant on paper. Privacy notices are drafted in clear language that maps to your actual data practices. Consumer request workflows include identity verification thresholds, SLA tracking, and escalation paths. Vendor contract amendments use templates that can be applied across your vendor portfolio. Retention policies include both the schedule and the disposal execution procedures so that retention limits are enforced, not just documented.
