The Readiness Sprint provides a rapid, structured assessment of your organization's current CCPA/CPRA compliance posture. Over two to four weeks, we map your personal information landscape, evaluate your consumer-facing privacy disclosures against the statute's requirements, and identify the gaps that carry the highest regulatory and litigation risk. The sprint is designed for organizations that need to understand where they stand before committing to a longer remediation program.

The engagement starts with a PI inventory workshop to identify all categories of personal information your organization collects, the sources, the business purposes, and the downstream recipients. From there, we assess your privacy notice and at-collection disclosures, test your consumer rights intake and fulfillment workflows, review a sample of vendor agreements for required CCPA/CPRA provisions, and evaluate your readiness for CPRA-specific obligations including sensitive personal information controls, data minimization, and the employee/applicant data extension. The output is an executive readout that ranks findings by risk and provides a clear remediation roadmap.
