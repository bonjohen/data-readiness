The Remediation Program closes the gaps identified in the Readiness Sprint by building the policies, processes, and technical infrastructure your AI governance program requires. Over 4-12 weeks, we work alongside your teams to develop governance policies tailored to your risk profile, implement model card programs, establish bias testing frameworks, and create the human oversight procedures that the NIST AI RMF requires.

Each deliverable is designed to be operational, not theoretical — policies include implementation guidance, templates are pre-populated with your system inventory, and procedures are tested against real workflows before handoff. The program's duration scales with the number of AI systems in scope and the maturity of your existing governance infrastructure.
