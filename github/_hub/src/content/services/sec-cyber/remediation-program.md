The Remediation Program closes the gaps identified in the Readiness Sprint by building the processes, documentation, and governance structures required for SEC cybersecurity disclosure compliance. Over 4-12 weeks, we develop your materiality determination playbook, establish 8-K filing procedures, design a board briefing program, and document your risk management framework in disclosure-ready language.

The program culminates in a tabletop exercise that simulates an end-to-end disclosure scenario — from incident detection through materiality determination to 8-K filing — testing whether your organization can meet the 4-business-day timeline with the people, processes, and documentation now in place.
