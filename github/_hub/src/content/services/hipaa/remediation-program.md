The Remediation Program closes the gaps identified in your readiness assessment by implementing the administrative, physical, and technical safeguards required by the HIPAA Security Rule. We work alongside your team to develop your risk analysis, deploy access controls and audit logging, draft and execute business associate agreements, and build contingency plans.

The program is structured in phases aligned to your risk priorities, ensuring the highest-severity gaps are addressed first. Each phase produces documentation and evidence artifacts ready for OCR review, so your compliance posture strengthens incrementally rather than waiting for a single go-live date.
