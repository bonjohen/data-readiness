The Readiness Sprint is a focused engagement that evaluates your organization's current HIPAA Security Rule posture and produces a clear path to compliance. In 2-4 weeks, we inventory your ePHI systems, map data flows, assess your existing risk analysis against OCR expectations, and review your business associate agreements for completeness.

You receive a prioritized remediation roadmap that ranks gaps by risk severity and OCR enforcement likelihood, giving your team a clear starting point whether you are preparing for an OCR audit, responding to a breach investigation, or pursuing compliance proactively.
