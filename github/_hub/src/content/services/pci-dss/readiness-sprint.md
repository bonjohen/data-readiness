The Readiness Sprint provides a comprehensive assessment of your current PCI DSS compliance posture in a compressed timeframe. We begin by defining the cardholder data environment boundary — identifying every system that stores, processes, or transmits cardholder data, and every system connected to those systems. This scoping exercise determines whether you need a Self-Assessment Questionnaire (SAQ) or a full Report on Compliance (ROC), and which SAQ type applies to your payment channels.

The PAN discovery scan identifies cardholder data in locations your team may not be aware of — file shares, email archives, development databases, log files, and backup media. Combined with the gap analysis against all 12 requirements, the output is a prioritized remediation roadmap that tells you exactly what to fix, in what order, and what level of effort each item requires before engaging a QSA for your formal assessment.
