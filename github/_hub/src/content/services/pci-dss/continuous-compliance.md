PCI DSS compliance is not a one-time achievement — it requires continuous maintenance of all controls throughout the year. The Continuous Compliance service provides the ongoing operational activities that many organizations struggle to sustain internally: quarterly vulnerability scans (both ASV and internal), daily log reviews, and annual penetration test coordination. We monitor your compliance posture month over month and alert you to drift before it becomes a finding.

The service also covers the administrative burden of keeping policies, procedures, and evidence current. When PCI SSC releases requirement updates, when your environment changes, or when your annual reassessment approaches, we ensure your documentation reflects reality and your evidence package is ready. This eliminates the last-minute scramble that makes PCI DSS assessments stressful and expensive.
