Continuous Compliance maintains your SHIELD Act security program as a living system rather than a point-in-time achievement. Monthly engagement ensures that safeguards evolve with your business, new risks are identified and addressed promptly, employee training stays current, and your breach notification procedures remain tested and ready. This ongoing relationship provides the sustained attention that the statute's reasonable security standard demands.
