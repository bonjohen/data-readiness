<h1>About</h1>

<div class="section">
  <h2>What We Do</h2>
  <p>We provide FTC Safeguards Rule readiness and compliance support for financial institutions required to maintain comprehensive information security programs. Our service helps owners, executives, and security leads navigate the path from "we need to comply with the amended rule" to "we have a documented, tested security program" with clear risk assessments, practical controls, and organized evidence.</p>

  <p>We work with mortgage brokers, motor vehicle dealers, finance companies, tax preparers, and other non-banking financial institutions under FTC jurisdiction &mdash; the institutions where Safeguards Rule compliance is mandatory and enforcement is active. See <a href="services">our service packages</a> for details on pricing and deliverables.</p>
</div>
