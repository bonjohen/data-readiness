<div class="section">
  <h2>Phase Details</h2>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>1. Intake &amp; Scoping <span class="phase-duration">Week 1</span></h3>
    <p>We start by understanding your current disclosure practices and governance structure.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Current disclosure practices review</strong> &mdash; assess existing cybersecurity disclosures in prior 10-K and 8-K filings</li>
      <li><strong>Incident response process evaluation</strong> &mdash; understand current IR processes and how they connect to disclosure decisions</li>
      <li><strong>Board governance structure assessment</strong> &mdash; map current board oversight of cybersecurity risk, including committee structures</li>
      <li><strong>Materiality determination process review</strong> &mdash; evaluate whether a defined materiality process exists and how it functions</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>2. Assessment <span class="phase-duration">Week 2&ndash;3</span></h3>
    <p>We evaluate your disclosure readiness against SEC requirements and emerging best practices.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>8-K readiness evaluation</strong> &mdash; assess ability to determine materiality and file within 4 business days</li>
      <li><strong>10-K disclosure gap analysis</strong> &mdash; compare current annual disclosures against SEC rule requirements</li>
      <li><strong>Board oversight documentation review</strong> &mdash; evaluate whether board cyber oversight is documented in disclosure-ready form</li>
      <li><strong>Management role and expertise assessment</strong> &mdash; assess how management's cyber role and expertise are described and evidenced</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>3. Outputs <span class="phase-duration">Week 3&ndash;4</span></h3>
    <p>We deliver the artifacts that establish your SEC disclosure readiness.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Materiality determination framework</strong> &mdash; defined process with criteria, roles, escalation paths, and documentation requirements</li>
      <li><strong>8-K filing procedures and templates</strong> &mdash; step-by-step procedures for incident-to-disclosure workflow</li>
      <li><strong>10-K disclosure language drafts</strong> &mdash; ready-to-file language for risk management, strategy, and governance sections</li>
      <li><strong>Board briefing framework</strong> &mdash; structured format for ongoing board cyber risk reporting</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>4. Follow-on <span class="phase-duration">Ongoing</span></h3>
    <p>After the readiness sprint, ongoing activities keep your disclosure program current.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Incident-to-disclosure playbook maintenance</strong> &mdash; update procedures as incidents occur and SEC guidance evolves</li>
      <li><strong>Board reporting cadence</strong> &mdash; support regular board cybersecurity briefings aligned to disclosure requirements</li>
      <li><strong>Annual 10-K disclosure updates</strong> &mdash; refresh risk management and governance language for each filing cycle</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
