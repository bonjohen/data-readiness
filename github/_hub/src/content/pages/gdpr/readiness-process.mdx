<div class="section">
  <h2>Phase Details</h2>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>1. Intake &amp; Scoping <span class="phase-duration">Week 1</span></h3>
    <p>We start by understanding your processing landscape, data flows, and compliance context.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Processing activity inventory</strong> &mdash; catalog all processing activities across the organization</li>
      <li><strong>Lawful basis mapping</strong> &mdash; identify the legal basis relied on for each processing activity</li>
      <li><strong>Third-party processor review</strong> &mdash; map all processors and sub-processors handling personal data</li>
      <li><strong>Cross-border transfer assessment</strong> &mdash; identify all international data flows and current transfer mechanisms</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>2. Assessment <span class="phase-duration">Week 2&ndash;3</span></h3>
    <p>We evaluate your current data protection posture against GDPR requirements.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>ROPA development</strong> &mdash; build or validate a comprehensive Record of Processing Activities</li>
      <li><strong>DPIA evaluation</strong> &mdash; identify processing activities requiring impact assessments and review existing DPIAs</li>
      <li><strong>Data subject rights workflow review</strong> &mdash; assess response procedures for all right types against 30-day timelines</li>
      <li><strong>International transfer mechanism assessment</strong> &mdash; evaluate adequacy of SCCs, BCRs, and other transfer safeguards</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>3. Outputs <span class="phase-duration">Week 3&ndash;4</span></h3>
    <p>We deliver the artifacts that define your path to GDPR accountability.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Gap register and remediation plan</strong> &mdash; every gap ranked by risk with owners and timelines</li>
      <li><strong>Privacy notice templates</strong> &mdash; GDPR-compliant notices for employees, customers, and website visitors</li>
      <li><strong>DPIA methodology framework</strong> &mdash; reusable methodology for conducting future impact assessments</li>
      <li><strong>Processor agreement templates</strong> &mdash; Article 28-compliant data processing agreement language</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>4. Follow-on <span class="phase-duration">Ongoing</span></h3>
    <p>After the readiness sprint, continued support ensures sustained compliance.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>ROPA maintenance</strong> &mdash; ongoing updates as processing activities change</li>
      <li><strong>DPIA updates for new processing</strong> &mdash; impact assessments for new products, features, and vendor relationships</li>
      <li><strong>Transfer impact assessments</strong> &mdash; ongoing evaluation of international transfer mechanisms</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
