<h1>About</h1>

<div class="section">
  <h2>What We Do</h2>
  <p>We provide GDPR operational readiness and compliance support for technology companies processing EU/EEA residents' personal data. Our service helps founders, CTOs, and privacy leads navigate the path from "we need GDPR compliance" to "we can demonstrate accountability" with documented processing activities, robust data subject rights procedures, and organized processor oversight.</p>

  <p>We work with small and mid-sized SaaS, AI, data, developer-tooling, and B2B software companies &mdash; the companies where GDPR readiness is the difference between accessing the EU market and being locked out of it. See <a href="../services">our service packages</a> for details on pricing and deliverables.</p>
</div>
