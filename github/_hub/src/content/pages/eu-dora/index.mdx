  <section class="content section">
    <h2>Why DORA Compliance Matters</h2>
    <p>The Digital Operational Resilience Act creates comprehensive ICT risk management requirements for the EU financial sector. Without a structured approach, organizations face enforcement action, supervisory scrutiny, and operational vulnerability.</p>
    <div class="value-grid">
      <div class="value-item">
        <h3>Meet EU Regulatory Requirements</h3>
        <p>DORA applies to virtually all EU financial entities and their critical ICT providers, creating enforceable requirements for ICT risk management, incident reporting, and resilience testing.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="value-item">
        <h3>Build Operational Resilience</h3>
        <p>DORA requires comprehensive ICT risk management and operational resilience testing, ensuring financial entities can withstand, respond to, and recover from ICT disruptions.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="value-item">
        <h3>Manage ICT Third-Party Risk</h3>
        <p>Critical ICT third-party providers face direct EU supervisory oversight under DORA, with requirements for concentration risk management and exit strategies.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="value-item">
        <h3>Prepare for Incident Reporting</h3>
        <p>DORA mandates harmonized ICT incident classification and reporting to competent authorities, requiring established processes and templates before incidents occur.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
