<div class="section">
  <h2>Phase Details</h2>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>1. Intake &amp; Scoping <span class="phase-duration">Week 1</span></h3>
    <p>We start by understanding your ICT environment and current resilience posture.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>ICT environment mapping</strong> &mdash; catalog ICT systems, infrastructure, and dependencies that support critical business functions</li>
      <li><strong>Current resilience practices review</strong> &mdash; assess existing ICT risk management, business continuity, and disaster recovery programs</li>
      <li><strong>Critical ICT provider identification</strong> &mdash; identify all ICT third-party providers and assess criticality levels</li>
      <li><strong>Regulatory classification determination</strong> &mdash; determine entity classification and applicable DORA requirements, including TLPT</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>2. Assessment <span class="phase-duration">Week 2&ndash;3</span></h3>
    <p>We evaluate your readiness against all five DORA pillars and regulatory technical standards.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>ICT risk management framework evaluation</strong> &mdash; assess current framework against DORA requirements for governance, protection, detection, and recovery</li>
      <li><strong>Incident reporting readiness assessment</strong> &mdash; evaluate ability to classify and report ICT incidents using harmonized templates</li>
      <li><strong>Resilience testing capability review</strong> &mdash; assess current testing practices against DORA testing requirements</li>
      <li><strong>Third-party ICT arrangement audit</strong> &mdash; review contractual arrangements and risk management for ICT providers</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>3. Outputs <span class="phase-duration">Week 3&ndash;4</span></h3>
    <p>We deliver the artifacts that establish your DORA compliance readiness.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>ICT risk management framework updates</strong> &mdash; gap remediation plan and updated framework aligned to DORA requirements</li>
      <li><strong>Incident classification and reporting procedures</strong> &mdash; harmonized templates and procedures for reporting to competent authorities</li>
      <li><strong>Resilience testing program design</strong> &mdash; testing strategy including scope, frequency, and TLPT roadmap for significant entities</li>
      <li><strong>Third-party ICT register and risk assessment</strong> &mdash; complete register of arrangements with concentration risk analysis and exit strategies</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>4. Follow-on <span class="phase-duration">Ongoing</span></h3>
    <p>After the readiness sprint, ongoing activities maintain DORA compliance and operational resilience.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>TLPT program execution</strong> &mdash; coordinate and support threat-led penetration testing for significant entities</li>
      <li><strong>Third-party ICT monitoring</strong> &mdash; ongoing assessment of ICT provider risk, concentration, and contract compliance</li>
      <li><strong>Incident reporting practice</strong> &mdash; tabletop exercises and process refinement for incident classification and reporting</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
