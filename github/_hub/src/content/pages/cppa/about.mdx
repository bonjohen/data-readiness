<h1>About</h1>

<div class="section">
  <h2>What We Do</h2>
  <p>We provide privacy readiness and compliance support for technology companies handling California residents' personal information. Our service helps founders, CTOs, and privacy leads navigate the path from "we need CCPA/CPRA compliance" to "we have a mature privacy program" with clear data maps, practical consumer rights procedures, and organized vendor oversight.</p>

  <p>We work with small and mid-sized SaaS, AI, data, developer-tooling, and B2B software companies &mdash; the companies where California privacy compliance is essential for market access and consumer trust. See <a href="../services">our service packages</a> for details on pricing and deliverables.</p>
