<div class="section">
  <h2>Phase Details</h2>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>1. Intake &amp; Scoping <span class="phase-duration">Week 1</span></h3>
    <p>We start by understanding your privacy program, data landscape, and compliance context.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Privacy program review</strong> &mdash; assess existing privacy policies, notices, and procedures</li>
      <li><strong>Data inventory kickoff</strong> &mdash; begin mapping personal information collection, use, and sharing</li>
      <li><strong>Vendor/contractor mapping</strong> &mdash; identify all third parties that receive or process personal information</li>
      <li><strong>Consumer rights workflow assessment</strong> &mdash; evaluate current ability to receive and respond to consumer requests</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>2. Assessment <span class="phase-duration">Week 2&ndash;3</span></h3>
    <p>We evaluate your current privacy posture against CCPA/CPRA requirements.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Data flow mapping</strong> &mdash; document how personal information moves through your systems, vendors, and partners</li>
      <li><strong>Privacy notice gap analysis</strong> &mdash; compare current notices against CPRA disclosure requirements</li>
      <li><strong>Consent mechanism review</strong> &mdash; assess opt-out, GPC signal handling, and SPI consent flows</li>
      <li><strong>SPI handling evaluation</strong> &mdash; review sensitive personal information practices against CPRA standards</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>3. Outputs <span class="phase-duration">Week 3&ndash;4</span></h3>
    <p>We deliver the artifacts that define your path to privacy compliance.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Privacy rights response procedures</strong> &mdash; documented workflows for handling all consumer request types</li>
      <li><strong>Vendor contract templates</strong> &mdash; CPRA-compliant service provider and contractor agreement language</li>
      <li><strong>Privacy impact assessment framework</strong> &mdash; methodology for evaluating high-risk processing activities</li>
      <li><strong>Remediation roadmap</strong> &mdash; prioritized plan to close gaps with owners and timelines</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>4. Follow-on <span class="phase-duration">Ongoing</span></h3>
    <p>After the readiness sprint, continued support ensures sustained compliance.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>GPC/opt-out implementation</strong> &mdash; technical implementation of Global Privacy Control and opt-out preference signals</li>
      <li><strong>CPRA audit compliance</strong> &mdash; preparation for cybersecurity audit requirements under CPRA regulations</li>
      <li><strong>Privacy notice updates</strong> &mdash; ongoing maintenance of privacy disclosures as regulations and practices evolve</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
