<div class="section">
  <h2>Phase Details</h2>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>1. Intake &amp; Scoping <span class="phase-duration">Week 1</span></h3>
    <p>We start by understanding your ePHI environment, existing safeguards, and business associate relationships.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>ePHI inventory and data flow mapping</strong> &mdash; identify where electronic protected health information is created, received, maintained, and transmitted</li>
      <li><strong>Current safeguards documentation review</strong> &mdash; assess existing policies, procedures, and technical controls</li>
      <li><strong>Business associate identification</strong> &mdash; catalog all vendors and subcontractors that handle ePHI</li>
      <li><strong>Risk analysis methodology selection</strong> &mdash; determine the approach for your comprehensive risk analysis</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>2. Assessment <span class="phase-duration">Week 2&ndash;3</span></h3>
    <p>We evaluate your current safeguards posture across all three Security Rule categories.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Administrative safeguards evaluation</strong> &mdash; review policies, training, incident procedures, contingency plans, and BAAs</li>
      <li><strong>Physical safeguards walkthrough</strong> &mdash; assess facility access, workstation security, and device controls</li>
      <li><strong>Technical controls assessment</strong> &mdash; evaluate access controls, audit logging, encryption, authentication, and transmission security</li>
      <li><strong>BAA completeness review</strong> &mdash; verify that all business associate agreements are current, complete, and properly executed</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>3. Outputs <span class="phase-duration">Week 3&ndash;4</span></h3>
    <p>We deliver the artifacts that define your path to HIPAA Security Rule compliance.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Risk analysis report</strong> &mdash; comprehensive threat and vulnerability assessment with risk ratings</li>
      <li><strong>Safeguards gap register</strong> &mdash; every safeguard mapped to Security Rule standards, with gaps ranked by risk</li>
      <li><strong>BAA templates and tracker</strong> &mdash; standardized BAA language and a tracking system for all business associate relationships</li>
      <li><strong>Remediation roadmap with risk ranking</strong> &mdash; prioritized plan to close gaps based on risk severity and implementation complexity</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>4. Follow-on <span class="phase-duration">Ongoing</span></h3>
    <p>After the readiness sprint, maintaining compliance requires ongoing attention to your security program.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Annual risk analysis updates</strong> &mdash; refresh the risk analysis to account for new threats, vulnerabilities, and system changes</li>
      <li><strong>Workforce security training</strong> &mdash; regular training programs with documented completion and comprehension</li>
      <li><strong>Contingency plan testing</strong> &mdash; periodic testing of backup, disaster recovery, and emergency mode operation plans</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
