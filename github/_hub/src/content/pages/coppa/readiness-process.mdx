<div class="section">
  <h2>Phase Details</h2>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>1. Intake &amp; Scoping <span class="phase-duration">Week 1</span></h3>
    <p>We start by understanding your product, audience, and current compliance posture.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Audience and directed-to analysis</strong> &mdash; determine whether your service is "directed to children" under COPPA's standards</li>
      <li><strong>Current consent mechanism review</strong> &mdash; evaluate existing parental consent flows and age-gating approaches</li>
      <li><strong>Third-party SDK/plugin audit</strong> &mdash; identify all third-party code that may collect children's personal information</li>
      <li><strong>Data collection inventory</strong> &mdash; catalog all personal information collected from users, especially those under 13</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>2. Assessment <span class="phase-duration">Week 2&ndash;3</span></h3>
    <p>We evaluate your current practices against COPPA requirements and FTC guidance.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Age gating mechanism evaluation</strong> &mdash; assess whether age screens meet FTC standards for neutrality and effectiveness</li>
      <li><strong>Parental consent flow assessment</strong> &mdash; review consent mechanisms against FTC-approved methods</li>
      <li><strong>Privacy notice compliance review</strong> &mdash; compare current notices against COPPA content and format requirements</li>
      <li><strong>Vendor COPPA obligation mapping</strong> &mdash; evaluate third-party responsibilities and contractual COPPA obligations</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>3. Outputs <span class="phase-duration">Week 3&ndash;4</span></h3>
    <p>We deliver the artifacts that define your path to COPPA compliance.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Consent mechanism design</strong> &mdash; recommended parental consent flow with implementation specifications</li>
      <li><strong>Privacy notice templates</strong> &mdash; COPPA-compliant online and direct notice templates</li>
      <li><strong>Vendor compliance requirements</strong> &mdash; contractual language and audit procedures for third-party data handlers</li>
      <li><strong>Data retention/deletion procedures</strong> &mdash; policies and technical procedures for children's data lifecycle management</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>4. Follow-on <span class="phase-duration">Ongoing</span></h3>
    <p>After the readiness sprint, continued support ensures sustained compliance.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Safe harbor program evaluation</strong> &mdash; assess eligibility for and benefits of FTC-approved safe harbor programs</li>
      <li><strong>Consent mechanism testing</strong> &mdash; ongoing verification that consent flows function as designed</li>
      <li><strong>FTC guidance monitoring</strong> &mdash; track regulatory updates, enforcement actions, and evolving FTC expectations</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
