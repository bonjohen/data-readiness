    <h2>Why State Privacy Law Compliance Matters</h2>
    <p>Companies operating across multiple US states face a growing patchwork of comprehensive privacy laws, each with unique requirements, thresholds, and enforcement mechanisms. Without a harmonized approach, compliance becomes fragmented, costly, and error-prone.</p>

      </div>

        <h3>Harmonize Compliance Programs</h3>
        <p>A single privacy program can address multiple state laws with the right framework, reducing duplication and ensuring consistent consumer protections across all jurisdictions.</p>

      <div class="value-item">

      </div>
      <div class="value-item">
