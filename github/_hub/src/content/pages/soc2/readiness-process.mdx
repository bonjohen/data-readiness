<div class="section">
  <h2>Phase Details</h2>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>1. Intake <span class="phase-duration">2&ndash;6 days</span></h3>
    <p>We start by understanding your business, systems, and compliance context.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>NDA and stakeholder mapping</strong> &mdash; identify the people who own systems, data, and processes</li>
      <li><strong>Document request</strong> &mdash; collect existing policies, architecture diagrams, vendor lists, and prior audit reports</li>
      <li><strong>Scoping interviews</strong> &mdash; understand your products, infrastructure, data flows, and customer commitments</li>
      <li><strong>System boundary draft</strong> &mdash; define the initial scope of the audit based on what we learn</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>2. Assessment <span class="phase-duration">9 days</span></h3>
    <p>We evaluate your current control posture against the selected Trust Services Criteria.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>TSC selection</strong> &mdash; recommend which criteria to include based on your business model and buyer expectations</li>
      <li><strong>Type 1 vs. Type 2 recommendation</strong> &mdash; determine the right report type for your timeline and goals</li>
      <li><strong>Control walkthroughs</strong> &mdash; review existing controls across all 12 domains (access, change management, logging, etc.)</li>
      <li><strong>Evidence sampling</strong> &mdash; assess what evidence you can already produce and identify gaps in your evidence repository</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>3. Outputs <span class="phase-duration">9 days</span></h3>
    <p>We deliver the artifacts that define your path to audit readiness.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Controls matrix and gap register</strong> &mdash; every control mapped to criteria, with gaps ranked by risk</li>
      <li><strong>Policy and document backlog</strong> &mdash; list of policies to write or update, prioritized by audit impact</li>
      <li><strong>Evidence calendar</strong> &mdash; what to collect, who collects it, and how often</li>
      <li><strong>Executive readout and remediation roadmap</strong> &mdash; board-ready summary with a prioritized plan to close gaps</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="phase-detail">
    <h3>4. Follow-on <span class="phase-duration">Variable</span></h3>
    <p>After the readiness sprint, the path forward depends on the gaps identified.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Remediation implementation</strong> &mdash; close gaps with our support (typically 1&ndash;4 months depending on maturity)</li>
      <li><strong>Type 2 observation period</strong> &mdash; if pursuing Type 2, operate controls for 3&ndash;12 months while collecting evidence</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
