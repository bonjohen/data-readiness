Incident response controls demonstrate that your organization can detect, contain, and recover from security incidents in an organized way. Auditors evaluate both the plan and evidence that it has been tested.

Even if you haven't had a real incident, a tabletop exercise provides strong evidence of process existence and team readiness. Document the scenario, participants, findings, and any improvements made.
