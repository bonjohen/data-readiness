Privacy controls apply when the Privacy Trust Services Criterion is in scope, typically for companies processing personal information subject to privacy commitments. This domain covers notice, consent, rights requests, and privacy-specific training.

Not every SOC 2 engagement includes Privacy. Include it when your product processes personal information in ways tied to privacy notices, consent flows, or rights-handling obligations. Consumer-facing products and B2B products with meaningful PII workflows are typical candidates.
