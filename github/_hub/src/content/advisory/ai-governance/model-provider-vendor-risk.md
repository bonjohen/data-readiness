The AI supply chain is uniquely concentrated — a small number of foundation model providers underpin a large share of enterprise AI applications. This creates risks that traditional vendor risk management does not adequately address: a single provider's policy change, outage, or model behavior shift can cascade across multiple business-critical applications simultaneously. Our advisory engagement helps organizations map their AI supply chain, quantify concentration risk, and develop contingency plans for provider disruptions.

We provide structured assessment frameworks for evaluating foundation model providers on dimensions specific to AI governance: training data transparency, bias testing disclosures, model card completeness, data handling practices, and incident response commitments. These assessments go beyond standard vendor security questionnaires to address the unique risks that AI vendors introduce.
