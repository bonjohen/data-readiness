The SEC's 4-business-day materiality determination timeline demands that companies can rapidly assess the scope and impact of cybersecurity incidents. When AI systems are involved — either as the target of an attack or as a vector for data exfiltration — the ability to reconstruct what happened depends on comprehensive logging of prompts, responses, and system interactions. Without these logs, materiality determination may be delayed or based on incomplete information.

Prompt and response logging also supports the broader risk management narrative disclosed in the 10-K. Companies that can demonstrate forensic capability across all systems, including AI, present a more credible picture of their cybersecurity risk management maturity.
