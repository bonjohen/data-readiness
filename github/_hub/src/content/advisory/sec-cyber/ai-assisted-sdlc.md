AI coding assistants and automated development pipelines introduce cybersecurity risks that may require disclosure under Item 106(b) if they are material to the company's risk profile. Organizations using AI in their SDLC should evaluate whether AI-generated code, model supply chain dependencies, or automated deployment decisions create risks that rise to the disclosure threshold — and document that evaluation as part of the risk management process described in the 10-K.

Including AI-assisted development controls in the cybersecurity risk management narrative strengthens the disclosure by demonstrating that the company proactively identifies emerging risk categories rather than limiting its assessment to traditional threats.
