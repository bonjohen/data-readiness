Enterprise data warehouses and analytics platforms aggregate data from across the organization, making them high-value targets whose compromise is more likely to cross materiality thresholds than breaches of individual systems. The cybersecurity risk management disclosure under Item 106(b) should address how the company protects these concentrated data assets, including access controls, monitoring, encryption, and segmentation.

Governance of analytics platforms is also relevant to board oversight disclosure. When the board reviews cybersecurity risk, warehouse and analytics security should be on the agenda — these systems often contain the financial, operational, and customer data that investors care about most.
