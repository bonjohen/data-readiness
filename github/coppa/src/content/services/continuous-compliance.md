COPPA compliance is not a one-time project. New features, vendor integrations, platform updates, and evolving FTC guidance create ongoing compliance obligations that require continuous attention. The Continuous Compliance service provides monthly monitoring and quarterly deep assessments to ensure your child-directed services remain compliant as your products and the regulatory landscape change.

Each quarter, we test your consent mechanisms for bypass vulnerabilities, re-assess third-party SDKs and vendors for COPPA compliance, review privacy notices against current collection practices, and validate that age gates are functioning as designed. We monitor FTC enforcement actions and rulemaking to alert you to changes that may affect your compliance posture, and we audit your parental deletion request process to ensure timely and complete responses.
