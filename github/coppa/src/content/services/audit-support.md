Audit Support provides expert coordination when your organization faces an FTC inquiry, safe harbor program audit, or internal compliance assessment. We manage the evidence assembly process, ensuring that consent transaction logs, privacy notice versions, vendor contracts, data retention records, and age gate documentation are organized, complete, and presented in a format that regulators and auditors expect.

Our team coaches control owners through the inquiry process, coordinates with safe harbor programs on your behalf, and conducts internal readiness reviews before any regulatory submission. Organizations use Audit Support to reduce the operational burden of regulatory response and to ensure that their compliance evidence tells a coherent, defensible story.
