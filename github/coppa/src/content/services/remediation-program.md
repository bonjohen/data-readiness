The Remediation Program closes the gaps identified in your Readiness Sprint. Over 4-12 weeks, we work alongside your product, engineering, and legal teams to implement compliant privacy notices, deploy or upgrade parental consent mechanisms, amend vendor contracts with COPPA-specific restrictions, build data retention and deletion workflows for children's PI, and redesign age gates to meet current FTC expectations.

Each deliverable is implemented to a standard that can withstand FTC scrutiny, with documentation, testing, and verification at every stage. The program includes internal training to ensure your teams understand COPPA obligations and can maintain compliance as products evolve. Organizations that have received FTC inquiries or are launching new child-directed services use the Remediation Program to move from gap analysis to demonstrable compliance.
