The Readiness Sprint delivers a comprehensive baseline assessment of your institution's compliance posture against the FTC Safeguards Rule as amended in 2023. In 2-4 weeks, we evaluate your qualified individual designation, customer information inventory, risk assessment maturity, and the nine specific safeguard categories required by § 314.4(c) — producing a clear picture of where you stand and what needs to happen next.

The engagement concludes with an executive readout that translates compliance gaps into business risk terms, with a prioritized remediation roadmap that accounts for regulatory urgency, implementation complexity, and available resources.
