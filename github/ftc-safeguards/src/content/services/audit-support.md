Audit Support provides expert coordination when your institution faces an FTC examination or third-party security assessment. We prepare your evidence package, ensure the qualified individual's board report is current and comprehensive, organize safeguard documentation across all control areas, and coordinate responses to examiner requests so your team can focus on their primary responsibilities.

The service includes pre-examination readiness reviews to identify and close documentation gaps before the assessor arrives, reducing the risk of findings that could have been prevented with better preparation.
