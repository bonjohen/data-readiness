SEC cybersecurity disclosure is not a one-time project — it requires ongoing maintenance of frameworks, documentation, and governance processes. Our Continuous Compliance engagement provides monthly support to keep your disclosure program current, including quarterly board briefing preparation, annual 10-K cybersecurity section updates, and regular reviews of your materiality framework as your business and threat landscape evolve.

We track regulatory developments — SEC staff guidance, enforcement actions, and comment letter trends — and assess their impact on your disclosure obligations, so your program stays ahead of evolving expectations rather than reacting to them.
