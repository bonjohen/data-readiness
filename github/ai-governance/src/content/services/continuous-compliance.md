AI governance is not a one-time exercise. Models drift, new AI systems are adopted, regulations evolve, and governance maturity must improve continuously. Our Continuous Compliance engagement provides the ongoing monitoring, review, and advisory support that keeps your AI governance program current and effective.

Monthly activities include bias monitoring reviews, performance drift analysis, and regulatory landscape updates. Quarterly reviews reassess your AI system inventory for new additions or changed risk profiles. Annually, we conduct a full governance maturity reassessment to measure progress and recalibrate the roadmap. This engagement is designed to supplement your internal governance capacity, not replace it — we build your team's capability while providing the specialized expertise that AI governance demands.
