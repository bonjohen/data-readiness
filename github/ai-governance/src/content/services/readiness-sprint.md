The Readiness Sprint produces a complete picture of your organization's AI governance posture in 2-4 weeks. We inventory every AI system in use — including third-party AI embedded in SaaS tools that teams may not think of as AI — classify each by risk tier, and assess your current controls against the full NIST AI RMF framework across GOVERN, MAP, MEASURE, and MANAGE functions.

You receive a gap analysis with prioritized remediation recommendations, a governance maturity score, and a roadmap that sequences remediation work by risk impact and implementation effort. The sprint is designed to give leadership a clear, evidence-based view of where the organization stands and what it will take to reach a defensible AI governance posture.
