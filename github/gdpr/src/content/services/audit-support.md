When a supervisory authority opens an inquiry — whether triggered by a complaint, a breach notification, or a sector-wide sweep — the quality and speed of your response directly affects the outcome. Our Audit & Inquiry Support engagement provides experienced GDPR practitioners who coordinate your response, assemble evidence packages that address the DPA's specific concerns, and review your ROPA and supporting documentation for completeness before submission. We have supported organizations through inquiries from multiple EU supervisory authorities and understand the procedural expectations of each.

This engagement also covers processor audit support under Art. 28(3)(h). Whether you are auditing your processors or being audited by your controller clients, we help structure the audit scope, prepare or review audit questionnaires, coordinate on-site or remote assessment logistics, and draft audit findings reports. For organizations facing their first DPA interaction, we provide guidance on procedural rights, response timelines, and the practical dynamics of supervisory authority engagement.
