Continuous Compliance provides ongoing monitoring and maintenance of your CCPA/CPRA compliance program. Privacy compliance is not a point-in-time achievement — new data collection practices, vendor relationships, product features, and regulatory guidance continuously create drift between your documented program and your actual practices. This engagement ensures that drift is detected and corrected on a monthly cadence.

Each month, we review consumer request metrics against your SLA commitments, flag overdue or at-risk requests, and validate that your PI inventory reflects current data flows. Quarterly deliverables include a full PI inventory refresh, vendor re-assessment against current contractual requirements, and a regulatory update briefing covering new CPPA rulemaking, enforcement actions, and AG opinions that may affect your program. The result is a compliance posture that remains current rather than decaying between annual reviews.
