Audit Support provides hands-on assistance when your organization faces a regulatory inquiry from the California Attorney General or the California Privacy Protection Agency, or when you need to prepare evidence for a compliance review. We coordinate across your legal, privacy, engineering, and HR teams to assemble responsive documentation, identify and remediate evidence gaps before they become findings, and prepare narrative responses that demonstrate your compliance posture.

The engagement scales to the inquiry's scope. For a focused consumer complaint, we help triage the allegations, assemble the relevant records, and draft a response. For a broader compliance review or investigative demand, we conduct an internal evidence audit, identify gaps, and work with your teams to close them before the response deadline. Our goal is to ensure that the documentation you produce accurately reflects your practices and meets the evidentiary standards regulators expect.
